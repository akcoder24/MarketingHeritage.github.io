About Us

Just as much as heritage is important marketing/advertising the heritage is important. Without proper or clean marketing of heritage, its values will be adulterated or simply lost in this adverse world.

We aim to find the marketing strategies of the famous heritage sites in today’s world and try to find out if the antiqueness of the place is preserved or it has been modified to the needs and the pace of the growing society. Based on these facts we try and debate on if marketing has been a pro or a con to these places. We also try and give certain marketing stratergies we fill will make a difference

Curated by the amature students of VIT Chennai, we hope to achieve our aims and help to discover the hidden places of India
